<Stats>
    Vitality - Max HP UP
    Endurance - Max Stamina UP; Equip Burden UP
    Strength - Meets Strength requirements for equipment; Increases damage on weapons with STR scaling
    Faith - Meets Faith requirements for equipment and miracles; Increases damage on weapons with FTH scaling; Magic Adjustment UP for most talismans; Magic Defense UP
</Stats>
<Stats-Info>
    HP - Hit points. When this reaches zero, the character dies.
    Stamina - Required for various actions.
    Physical Defense - Defense against physical attacks.
    Magic Defense - Defense against magic attacks.
</Stats-Info>
